# EA-001 — Chapter 4
# Constitutional Objects of the Universal Engineering Operating System (CO-UEOS)

## Purpose

The Constitutional Objects define the canonical engineering concepts of the
BIP Universal Engineering Operating System (UEOS). Every subsystem owns exactly
one Constitutional Object.

---

## CO-001 — Engineering Knowledge

**Owner:** Engineering Knowledge System (EKS)

Engineering knowledge curated by engineers including architecture,
standards, patterns, research, best practices, and lessons learned.

---

## CO-002 — Engineering Evidence

**Owner:** Engineering Audit System (EAuS)

Observed engineering facts collected from reality.

Properties:

- Immutable
- Traceable
- Timestamped
- Verifiable
- Repeatable

---

## CO-003 — Engineering Truth

**Owner:** Engineering Registry System (ERS)

Governed engineering truth established from validated engineering evidence.

Engineering Truth shall never originate from opinion.

---

## CO-004 — Unified Engineering Graph

**Owner:** Engineering Compiler System (ECS)

The canonical runtime representation of governed Engineering Truth.

---

## CO-005 — Engineering View

**Owner:** Engineering Publishing Framework (EPF)

Governed projections of the Unified Engineering Graph.

Examples:

- CER
- ERMS
- EA
- ADR
- Reports
- Dashboards
- REST APIs
- PDF
- Markdown

---

## CO-006 — Engineering Capability

**Owner:** Engineering Runtime System (ERTS)

Executable engineering functionality provided by UEOS.

---

## CO-007 — Engineering Competency

**Owner:** Engineering Academy System (EAS)

Validated engineering proficiency demonstrated through assessment and practice.

---

---

# Constitutional Rules

### Rule 001

Every UEOS subsystem SHALL own exactly one Constitutional Object.

### Rule 002

Every Constitutional Object SHALL have exactly one owning subsystem.

### Rule 003

Engineering Truth SHALL originate only from validated Engineering Evidence.

### Rule 004

The Unified Engineering Graph SHALL be the canonical runtime representation of Engineering Truth.

### Rule 005

Engineering Views SHALL project Engineering Truth and SHALL NOT create or modify it.

### Rule 006

Engineering Capabilities SHALL consume Constitutional Objects but SHALL NOT redefine them.

### Rule 007

Every Engineering Artifact SHALL be traceable to Engineering Truth, and every Engineering Truth SHALL be traceable to Engineering Evidence.
