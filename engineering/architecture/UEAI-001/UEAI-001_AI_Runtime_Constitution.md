# Chapter 01 --- Mission

The UEOS AI Runtime provides the intelligence layer of the Unified
Engineering Operating System (UEOS).

Its mission is to enable AI-native engineering through governed,
transparent, explainable, and extensible system services.

The AI Runtime shall:

-   Understand engineering context.
-   Reason over repository knowledge.
-   Plan engineering work.
-   Execute approved workflows.
-   Coordinate specialized agents.
-   Continuously improve engineering processes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chapter 02 --- Constitutional Principles

The AI Runtime is governed by ten constitutional principles:

1.  AI is a System Service.
2.  AI Never Acts Without Context.
3.  AI Uses Tools.
4.  AI Explains Decisions.
5.  Planning Before Execution.
6.  Engineering Over Code.
7.  Governed Autonomy.
8.  Everything is Observable.
9.  Provider Independence.
10. Continuous Engineering.

These principles apply to every AI subsystem.

# Chapter 04 --- Core Subsystems

## AI Kernel

Central orchestration point for every AI interaction.

## Context Engine

Constructs repository-aware engineering context.

## Memory Manager

Stores persistent engineering knowledge and preferences.

## Prompt Engine

Renders versioned prompts from reusable templates.

## Tool Registry

Provides governed access to engineering tools.

## Decision Engine

Selects the next engineering action based on context and policy.

## Planning Engine

Transforms goals into executable engineering plans.

## Workflow Runtime

Executes plans with dependency management, retries, and observability.

## Multi-Agent Framework

Coordinates specialized engineering agents.

## Engineering Intelligence

Provides repository reasoning, architecture analysis, and engineering
recommendations.

# Chapter 05 --- Engineering Lifecycle

Every engineering request follows this lifecycle:

1.  Observe
2.  Understand
3.  Collect Context
4.  Plan
5.  Validate
6.  Execute
7.  Verify
8.  Learn
9.  Record

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chapter 06 --- Governance

Every AI action should be:

-   Explainable
-   Auditable
-   Reproducible
-   Policy compliant
-   Observable
-   Reversible when practical

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chapter 07 --- Self-Evolution

UEOS is designed to be self-evolving rather than self-mutating.

The AI Runtime may:

-   Detect improvement opportunities.
-   Generate implementation plans.
-   Produce ADRs.
-   Execute approved workflows.
-   Benchmark outcomes.
-   Recommend future improvements.

All autonomous behavior remains subject to governance policies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chapter 08 --- Long-Term Vision

UEOS is an AI-native Engineering Operating System capable of designing,
building, validating, operating, and continuously improving software
systems.

The AI Runtime is a constitutional subsystem that transforms engineering
knowledge into governed engineering execution.
