## Engineering Rule

Implementation artifacts remain in their functional locations.

Canonical engineering knowledge resides in CER.

ERMS is the authoritative source of engineering truth.

---

## Ecosystem Layers

1. Engineering
2. Academy
3. Artificial Intelligence
4. Products

These domains consume and contribute Canonical Engineering
Records through the Engineering Record Management System.

---

## Bootstrap Principle

Bootstrap generates:

- Engineering Records
- Source Code
- Tests
- Reports
- Academy Content
- AI Knowledge
- Product Templates

Every generated engineering document becomes a Canonical
Engineering Record.

---

## Guiding Principle

Knowledge Before Code.

Architecture Before Implementation.

Governance Before Growth.

Automation Before Repetition.

Engineering Before Everything.
